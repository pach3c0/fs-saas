Iconography & logo

Two drawing systems coexist. The logo is a single wordmark with a lens-mark dot. Icons come from one library (Lucide), one weight, one size class. Imagery is always a placeholder until the photographer uploads their own — we never ship stock.

3.1 Logo

CliqueZoom wordmark · one lockup, three sizes.

Wordmark in Playfair 700 with a small circular "lens" mark replacing the dot. The italic tagline "em 1 clique" is optional and only appears in marketing contexts at XL.

Clear space & minimums

Clear space
At least the height of the lens-mark on all sides. Never crop the mark with a container edge.
Minimum size
Wordmark 14px on screen / 12pt in print. Below that, use the mark alone.
On photography
Always white, with text-shadow: 0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.4). If the photo is busy, place a 60%-opacity black pill behind it.

Misuse

CliqueZoom No gradient backgrounds
CliqueZoom Never recolor wordmark
CliqueZoom No font substitution
CliqueZoom No skew, no rotate, no stretch
3.2 Icons

Lucide. 1.5px stroke. Outline only.

One library, one weight. We use Lucide outline at a 1.5px stroke. Filled icons are reserved for selected states (e.g. a star on a favorited gallery). Don't mix libraries; don't mix weights.

3.3 Imagery & placeholders

Empty states use checkerboards, not stock photos.

CliqueZoom is a home for a photographer's own work. We refuse to ship stock. In our admin and marketing UI, image slots show a neutral checkerboard with the aspect ratio labelled — this signals "your photo goes here" without putting a stranger's face on someone's portfolio.

Aspect ratios in play

Hero banners
16:9 on marketing; 21:9 on photographer Escuro theme.
Gallery thumbs
Masonry (variable heights) for Galeria; 1:1 grid for Moderno and Minimalista; 3:2 for Elegante.
Testimonial avatars
1:1 circular, 48px on marketing, 40px on admin.
Preview iframes
Builder previews use 16:10 at 1.0× zoom, scaled to fit.
Favicon / app icon
1024×1024 master → exports to 16/32/180/192/512. Mark only, no wordmark.
