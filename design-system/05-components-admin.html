5.9 Do & don't

What we reject in review.

PatternStatusWhy
Admin in light modeRejectDark-first is a product decision. A light-mode toggle is a future roadmap item, not today.
Playfair headlines in adminRejectInter only in admin. Serif is for photographer-facing surfaces.
Colorful iconographyRejectIcons are var(--ad-ink-2). Color is reserved for status.
"Dashboard" / "Overview" ambiguityRejectOne label per concept. See Content lexicon.
Rounded 12px+ cardsReject6px default. 8px on modals. Bigger radii belong to marketing.
Emoji in status pillsRejectDots and labels only. Emoji break tabular rhythm.
