Marketing kit

Every component on the public-facing site (cliquezoom.com.br), the signup flow, the pricing page, and the post-login onboarding. Editorial — not enterprise.

4.1 Buttons

Four variants. One primary per view.

The primary is black, period. The secondary is the outlined version of the primary. The ghost is for tertiary actions in dense UIs. The link button is for in-flow clickable text.

4.9 Don't

Common mistakes we reject in review.

PatternStatusWhy
Two primary buttons side-by-sideRejectOne action per view. Use secondary for the alternate.
Gradient text on body copyRejectReserved for occasional display headlines on photographer sites only.
Filled icons in marketing UIRejectOutline-only. Filled is admin-only for status.
Card with both shadow AND thick borderRejectPick one. Hairline border at rest, shadow on hover.
"Get started free!!!" copyRejectEditorial voice — no marketing exclamations. See Content.
Stock photography of generic peopleRejectEither real customer photography or checkerboard placeholder.
"Buy now" / "Click here"RejectUse the action verb. "Começar grátis", "Ver planos", "Falar com vendas".
